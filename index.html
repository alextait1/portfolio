About Me

A little bit about where I'm coming from and where I hope to go

How I Found Code

My web development journey began with building and maintaining a few Squarespace websites for bands I was in. In doing thatlimitations of the basic platform, and wanted more control over the code. I started doing online coding turorials like Skill Crush and Code Academy and got super into it! I enroled in Hacker You's part-time web development class in the fall of 2016 and that was the game changer for me. I felt so empowered and excieted by the things I was learning and applying, and I was hungry for more.

I wrapped up my music teaching business permanently in the spring of 2017. As of July was attending Hacker You's full-time immersive web development bootcamp, which was just the kickstart I needed to hone my skills and get fully hooked on coding!

Interests

I am highly profficient with HTML and CSS and my JavaScript/ReactJS are getting stronger every day. In terms of my long-term career vision, I am very interested in UX/UI and product design and development. My dream job would be on a team with great culture, where my exisiting skillset would be useful and where I would have some room to grow into new skills too.

I'm currently seeking a full-time front-end web development position here in Toronto for the fall of 2017.
